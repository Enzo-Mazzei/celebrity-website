Damso is a Belgian rapper of Congolese origin. Known for his distinctive rap style and introspective lyrics, he gained widespread recognition with his breakthrough album "Batterie Faible" in 2016. Damso's music often tackles themes such as love, relationships, and personal struggles, resonating with a diverse audience. He has since released several successful albums, solidifying his position as one of the most influential French-speaking rappers in recent years.
